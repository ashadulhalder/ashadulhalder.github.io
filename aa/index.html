About Spectrum 2025

Part of the rich legacy of the Post Graduate and Research Department of Physics, St. Xavier’s College (Autonomous), Kolkata has been celebrating and intermingling with Spectrum, the annual fest conducted by the department for the past twelve years.

Put together the remarkable and ever-growing efforts, the bombilating energy, and the enthralling passion of every student of our department and you will get something as magnificent and spectacular as Spectrum, a new version every single year. We are happy to state that Spectrum is back in all its glory, mightier and more notable than it has ever been.

The theme for our fest this year is “Ripples in Time.” We will not only explore fascinating vistas and remarkable concepts but we will also be launching “Horizon”, our departmental magazine. Spectrum consists of a plethora of events each of which holds utmost significance in view of our rapidly evolving world.

We, at the Department of Physics extend our heartfelt gratitude to all those who would participate as well as to all the spectators in the elevation Spectrum 2025 is aiming to scale.
