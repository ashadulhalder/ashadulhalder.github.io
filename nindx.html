Natural Unit Conversion



      Natural Unit         MKSA Unit    
     
Length 1/eV = m
Mass eV = kg
Time 1/eV = s
Frequency eV = Hz
Force eV 2 = N
Energy eV = J
Energy Density (ε) eV4 = N/m3
Pressure (P) eV4 = Pascal
Charge = C
Magnetic induction (B) eV2 = Tesla
Electric potential (φ) eV = V

The natural unit system is widely used in the fields of particle physics, defined by c=ℏ=kB=1.
Where c is the speed of light, ℏ is the reduced Planck constant and kB is the Boltzmann constant.
